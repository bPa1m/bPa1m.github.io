# BloodFort Is Created!
The start of something new, I have started production work on a new game called BloodFort. Heavily inspired by games like Left 4 Dead, and Project Zomboid; I seek to capture the essence of base defense and zombie survival shooters in an action-packed and strategic experience called BloodFort.

I look forward to sharing more about this new game as progress comes along!
